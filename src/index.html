ABOUT LEAGUE OF LEGENDS



League of Legends is a team strategy game in which two teams of five champions face off to see who destroys before the other's base. Choose from a cast of 140 champions to make epic plays, assassinate opponents and take down turrets to rise to victory.


Considering there are over 140 champions, it won't take long to find your style of play. Dominate one or all.
